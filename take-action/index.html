Take Action

NumFOCUS has the mission of supporting open code for the betterment of science. This goal requires YOU! Whether you write code or just want to support the cause there are numerous ways to get started.

Ways to Support NumFOCUS

  • Join NumFOCUS — By joining our ranks you will be contributing to a large community of core committers.
  • Donate to NumFOCUS — Your money will go to help support projects that are critical to the success of science.
  • Become a Corporate Sponsor — Have your company support the tools they depend on!
  • Join the Discussion — Interact with the community via webforums and social media.
